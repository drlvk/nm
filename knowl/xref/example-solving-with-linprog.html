Example 35.1.1. A simple linear programming problem.

Maximize \(x_1+x_2\) subject to constraints \(x_1 + 2x_2 \le 4\) and \(3x_1 + x_2 \le 5\text{.}\)
🔗
Solution.
Rephrasing this as the minimization of \(-x_1-x_2\text{,}\) we see that the coefficient vector should be [-1 -1]. Executing
linprog([-1; -1], [1 2; 3 1], [4; 5])
yields [1.2; 1.4]. (Try to visualize this problem and its solution.)
