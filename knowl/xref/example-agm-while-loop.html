Example 4.2.1. Arithmetic-geometric mean using a while loop.

Find the arithmetic-geometric mean (AGM) of two numbers given by a user, with 1e-9 accuracy.
🔗
Answer.
x = input('x = ');
y = input('y = ');
while abs(x-y) >= 1e-9
    a = (x+y)/2;
    g = sqrt(x*y);
    x = a;
    y = g;
end
fprintf('AGM(x, y) = %.9f\n', x)
